Meditation Techniques

These simple meditation techniques can help reduce stress, improve focus, and support your overall mental health.

Deep Breathing

Breathe in slowly through your nose, hold for a few seconds, and breathe out slowly. This helps calm your body and reduce stress quickly.

Mindfulness Meditation

Focus on the present moment and your breathing. If your mind wanders, gently bring your attention back without judgment.

Body Scan

Close your eyes and focus on each part of your body from head to toe, relaxing any tension you feel.

Guided Meditation

Listen to a voice or audio that walks you through relaxing your body and mind step by step.

Visualization

Imagine a peaceful place like a beach or forest and focus on the sights, sounds, and feelings.
